Members of Nabwes

All student of indigene of Badagry west are automatically the member of the National Association of Badagry West Student (Nabwes). The membership extends to all secondary school students of badagry west indigen

Tertiary institution students within and diaspora shall be members of the National Association of Badagry west student and shall be made to pay the sum of N1,000 (annual due) or as otherwise stated by the SPC..

A student on suspension from any tertiary institution shall remain a bonafide member of NABWES

A withdrawn or dismissed student shall automatically forfeit his /her rights and claims to the membership of NABWES
